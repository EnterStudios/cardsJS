Hand Layouts

Cards can be grouped into a hand. A hand is an <ol class='hand'> followed by card list item(s) (<li><img cid=".."></li>). When the mouse is over a card in an hand, the card is moved veritically or horizontally to indicate that it will be selected.

Horizontal Hand

A simple horizontal layout of a hand. Every card is completely visible. Hovering over the card will make it jump up.
